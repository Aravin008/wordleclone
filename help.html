How to play

  • You have to guess 5 letter word.
  • You have 6 chances to guess the word.
  • Upon guessing the word you will get the clues as follows:
    • A - You have guessed correct letter and placed in correct position.
    • A - You have guessed correct letter but it is placed in wrong position.
    • A - The letter you guessed is not part of the word.
